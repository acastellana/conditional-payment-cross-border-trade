Trade Lifecycle

From invoice to settlement

Five steps. One contract. If shipment timing is contested, GenLayer resolves the disputed fact — and the same settlement engine continues.

1
DRAFT
Invoice Created
150,000 BOB trade registered on-chain. Terms agreed between exporter and importer.
2
RATE LOCKED
Benchmark Locked
GenLayer validators agree on 0.493 PEN/BOB. Rate delivered to Base and recorded on-chain.
3
SETTLEMENT COMPUTED
Settlement Amount Computed
150,000 × 0.493 = 73,950 PEN. Awaiting shipment check before final release.
4
SHIPMENT CHECK
Shipment Checked
If uncontested, settlement proceeds. If the importer contests timing within the 7-day window, GenLayer evaluates the evidence and returns a verdict. The contract maps that verdict to release, refund, or manual review.
Shipment accepted. Settlement proceeds to final release.

InternetCourt · shipment-deadline-v1
"Shipment under Contract QC-COOP-2026-0004 crossed Bolivian export customs at Desaguadero on or before 2026-04-05 23:59 America/La_Paz."
What happened
Importer contested timing. Exporter presented a pre-clearance stamp. Importer presented a gate record showing physical crossing four days after deadline.
What GenLayer found
LATE SHIPMENT
Pre-clearance stamp is not a crossing event. Gate record shows crossing on April 9 — four days late.
What the contract did
Settlement cancelled. 73,950 PEN refunded to importer.

InternetCourt · shipment-deadline-v1
"Shipment under Contract QC-COOP-2026-0005 crossed Bolivian export customs at Desaguadero on or before 2026-04-05 23:59 America/La_Paz."
What happened
Importer contested timing. Both documents had illegible fields — one couldn't be matched to this shipment, the other had an ambiguous date.
What GenLayer found
UNDETERMINED
Neither document yielded a verifiable crossing timestamp. Evidence insufficient.
What the contract did
Settlement frozen. 14-day manual review window opened. If no ruling, defaults to refund.

Why GenLayer Matters Here

The distinction that matters

What a normal oracle can do
Lock a benchmark rate and return a number.
What a normal oracle cannot do
Resolve which shipment record controls when real-world evidence conflicts.
What GenLayer does
Evaluates the evidence, returns a machine-usable verdict, and lets the same contract continue automatically.
