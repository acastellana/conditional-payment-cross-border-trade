Trust model

Benchmark path (relayer)

GenLayer validators agree on the rate. Relayer calls receiveRate() on Base. Protected by onlyRelayer. The rate is a public market number — verifiable by anyone.

Verdict path (bridge)

GenLayer evaluates shipment evidence. Delivery via BridgeSender → BridgeForwarder → LayerZero V2 → BridgeReceiver → Factory. Protected by onlyVerdictSource. Relayer cannot forge a verdict.
